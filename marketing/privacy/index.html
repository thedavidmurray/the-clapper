Privacy Policy

The Clapper for iOS · Effective 2026-05-25

Summary

The Clapper does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information. All audio analysis happens on your device. Photos and videos captured through the app are saved directly to your Photo Library and never sent anywhere by The Clapper.

What the app accesses

  • Microphone — to listen for claps, snaps, and other audio gestures in real time. Audio is processed as a live stream by Apple's on-device SoundAnalysis framework and discarded immediately. No audio recordings are saved by The Clapper.
  • Camera — to record video when triggered by a gesture or the manual record button.
  • Photo Library (add only) — to save videos and photos you capture in the app. The Clapper cannot read or browse your existing library.

Data collection

None. The Clapper has no analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, no crash reporter, no third-party network requests, and no user accounts. The app does not make any network requests in normal operation.

Data sharing

Nothing is shared with anyone. Apple's App Store may collect standard download and crash metrics through the operating system; that is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours.

Children

The Clapper is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. It does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If we change this policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
