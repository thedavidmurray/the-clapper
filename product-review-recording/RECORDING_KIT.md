# Clapper App Review Recording Kit

Apple requires a demo video on a physical iOS device for App Review appeal.

## Quickstart
1. Open The Clapper on a real iPhone/iPad.
2. Use built-in screen recorder (Settings > Control Center > Screen Recording).
3. Record the clips below.
4. Export and upload to YouTube unlisted or Google Drive shareable link.
5. Update EDGA-8502 (upload + App Store Connect) and reference the link.

## Shot List

- 01 app launch            ~15s
- 02 permissions grant     ~30s
- 03 clap detection demo   ~45s
- 04 remote control        ~30s
- 05 settings/config       ~20s

Total target: 2-3 minutes.

See shotlist.csv for details.
