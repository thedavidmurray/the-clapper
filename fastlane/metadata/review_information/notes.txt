This app requires microphone access for audio gesture detection (claps, snaps) and camera access for video recording. The app uses Apple's built-in SoundAnalysis framework (SNClassifySoundRequest with .version1 classifier) for on-device ML classification. No custom ML models are included. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted for detection. No account or sign-in required.

To test:
1. Launch the app
2. Grant microphone permission when prompted
3. From the Home tab, tap "Start Listening"
4. Clap twice — the app will detect the double clap gesture and toggle video recording
5. Navigate to the Camera tab to test gesture-triggered recording with the camera permission
6. Settings tab lets the reviewer remap any gesture to any action

No demo account needed.
